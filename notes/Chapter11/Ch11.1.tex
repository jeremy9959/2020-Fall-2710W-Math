\textbf{Examples of Relations}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  \(=\), \(<\), \(>\), \(\le\), \(\ge\), \(\not=\), \emph{etc.} are
  relations between numbers. \vfill
\item
  \(\subseteq\) is a relation between sets
\end{itemize}

\vfill\eject

\textbf{More examples} - ``is the parent of'' or ``is a child of'' or
``is a spouse of'' are relations between people. \vfill - ``comes
earlier in the dictionary'' is a relation between words. \vfill\eject
