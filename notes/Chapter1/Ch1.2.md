## Definition

**Definition:** The Cartesian Product $A\times B$ of two sets $A$ and $B$ is the set of ordered pairs
$(a,b)$ where $a\in A$ and $b\in B$.
$$
A\times B =\{(a,b):a\in A, b\in B\}
$$
